Srijan Labs operates lean but thinks big. The focus is not on building apps — it is on building operating systems for businesses.

Quicksy is a WhatsApp-first hyperlocal ordering and automation platform built to simplify how neighborhood businesses accept, process, and fulfill orders.

What Quicksy Does

Enables customers to place orders directly via WhatsApp

Converts natural language into structured orders

Manages SKU mapping, pricing, and brand variations

Automates order confirmation and tracking

Reduces manual errors and operational friction

Strategic Vision

Quicksy is evolving into a commerce layer for neighborhood brands, a GT digitization tool for FMCG, and a transaction intelligence engine for small businesses.
